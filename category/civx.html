Recent Projects: Knowledge

Tue 07 August 2012
By Nathaniel Case

In CIVX.

tags: knowledge

One of the cool features of CIVX that really caught my interest when I first started working on it was the Knowledge DB. Using the magic of Python, Knowledge is a vertical, polymorphic databse for storing knowledge about things, which means very little to anyone, even me.

What Knowledge does is it allows you to store anything (or anything Python can pickle, at least), as arbitrary collections of Entities and Facts.

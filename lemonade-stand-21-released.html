Lemonade Stand 2.1 Released

Fri 12 November 2010
By Nathaniel Case

In FOSS@RIT.

tags: lemonade

I am proud to announce the immediate availability of Lemonade Stand 2.1, with a quarter's worth of work and effort behind it. There are few gameplay changes in this release, but it feels like a brand new game thanks to the wonderful new art assets courtesy of JT. There's also the beginning of a help system, and many generalizations in the code to make adding new features even easier than before. We're a little late for a midnight release, but for my first real game release, I don't think we did half bad. http://activities.sugarlabs.org/en-US/sugar/addon/4321

pyDex 1.0

Sun 08 May 2011
By Katherine Case

In personal.

tags: pyDex

And in mostly unrelated but terribly belated news, I finally released pyDex 1.0 this morning. Black/White pokedex, new config system, baby pokemon, and game detection, are all new features introduced in this version. Old configs should get read properly and get seamlessly updated to the new version, though there may be a few bugs in the process.
